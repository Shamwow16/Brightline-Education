Our Goals

Brightline's goal is to help rural girls and women develop the science, math and English-language skills necessary to attend university. We assess progress through our tablet technology, which allows students to work through their daily lessons, and through the feedback of our Brightline Ambassadors in the field, who log daily reports noting the progress students are making.

Short-Term Goals: Brightline will start by working with a small, concentrated group of girls in kindergarten through 5th grade, providing them with one on one education with the aid of the tablets, to instructing them in math, science, and English.

Long-Term Goal: Brightline aspires to expand its programs beyond the borders of a single country and to individuals lacking access to a basic education no matter their age or gender.
