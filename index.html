"If you educate a man, you educate an individual, but if you educate a woman, you educate a whole family."

- Jawahrlal Nehru

Education is a basic human right enshrined in international law that empowers economically and socially marginalized individuals to lift themselves out of poverty.

For girls and young women in particular, education provides the means by which they can protect themselves from exploitation and positively impact their societies.

By supporting Brightline Education, you directly support the education of girls in rural communities across the world.
